How it Works

Four simple steps to complete legal clarity.

1

Upload & Read

Upload your PDF contract. Our system securely parses the document structure and extracts the raw text.

2

Privacy Masking

Sensitive information like Aadhaar, IFSC, names, and phone numbers are automatically masked locally before analysis.

3

Clause Splitting & Retrieval

The document is broken down into clauses. Relevant legal context is retrieved from our FAISS vector database.

4

AI Analysis

An advanced LLM individually analyzes each clause for risk, legality, and references applicable laws.
